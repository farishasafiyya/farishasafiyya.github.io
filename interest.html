First Interest: TREASURE

Previous Leaders: Hyunsuk, Jihoon

TREASURE changed their leaders in 2025 as part of a rotation system. Junkyu and Asahi replaced Hyunsuk and Jihoon to allow new leadership growth. No issues involved — just team development.
